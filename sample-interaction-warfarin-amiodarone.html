Can I take Warfarin with Amiodarone?

Use caution — this combination needs review. Major

Warfarin combined with a medicine that raises its level

What can happen

These medicines slow the breakdown of warfarin, which can push your INR up and increase bleeding risk — sometimes days after starting.

Why it happens

Inhibition of CYP2C9 (and CYP3A4) clearance of warfarin raises exposure; amiodarone also acts via a long-lived metabolite, so onset is delayed.

What to do

Your prescriber may want to check your INR more often while these overlap. Don't change your warfarin dose yourself.
