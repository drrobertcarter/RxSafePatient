When should I take Levothyroxine?

Levothyroxine is usually taken on an empty stomach in the morning — about 30–60 minutes before food, coffee, calcium, or iron — at the same time each day.

Keep Levothyroxine at least 4 hours away from calcium, iron, dairy, and antacids — they block its absorption.

Can I take Levothyroxine at night?
